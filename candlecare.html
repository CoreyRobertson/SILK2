Candle Care

The first time you light your soy candle, be sure to let your wax form a melt pool that reaches the edges of your vessel. This pool will leave a memory for your candle to avoid tunneling. Always trim your candle wick 5mm prior to each burn using either a wick trimmer, nail clippers or your finger tips. This will give your candle a clean, fresh burn. Be careful to not leave any wick trimmings in the wax pool.

Burn your candle for no more than 3-4 hours at a time, making sure the melt pool has reached the sides of the vessel. It is always best to use a candle snuffer or wick dipper rather than blowing out the flame. This will help prevent splashing of the wax, setting the wick off-centre, the production of sooty residue and will help maximise the burn time of the candle.

As we do not use any additives or setting agents in our candles, the reset after the first burn may not leave the smooth top that the candle started with. This is due to the nature of our Kosher wax, that is used specifically for greater fragrance performance.

Discontinue use when your wax reaches 1-1.5cm from the bottom of your vessel. Please do not burn your vessel dry, this will result in cracking or exploding glassware. Never place your candle in porous or flammable panels. Avoid burning your candle in a drafted area including windows, vents or fans. Keep out of reach of childre and pets, and please, never leave an ignited candle unattended.
